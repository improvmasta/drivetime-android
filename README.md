# drivetime

### Every drive, logged automatically. Every mile, yours to keep.

drivetime turns your phone into an effortless driving journal. Get in the car and it
starts. Arrive and it stops. No buttons. No *"did I remember to track that trip?"* Just a
complete, accurate history of everywhere you drove — miles, times, and routes already added
up for you.

And it runs **entirely on your phone.** No account. No login. No cloud you're renting space
in. Your driving history lives on your device and answers only to you.

---

## Set it once. Forget it forever.

Most trip trackers have one job — to actually be running when you drive — and that's exactly
where they quietly fail. drivetime is built around a single promise:

> **It never forgets a trip.**

It survives reboots, updates, dead zones, and the aggressive battery-killers on Samsung and
Xiaomi phones. If the system tries to shut it down, it comes right back. You will never open
the app to find a drive missing.

---

## What it does for you

🚗 **Starts itself — in any car.**
No "start trip" button. The moment you begin moving, drivetime wakes and starts logging
within seconds. Connect to your car stereo and it *knows* you're driving. Borrow a car or
rent one? A motion sensor catches the start anyway.

🔋 **Sips battery when you're parked.**
All day it runs a whisper-quiet background trace — enough to catch your next drive, gentle
enough you'll never spot it in your battery stats. It only ramps up to precise, second-by-
second tracking once you're actually on the road.

📍 **Mark a stop without unlocking your phone.**
Pull up to a job site, a client, a delivery — tap **Mark** right on the lock-screen
notification and name it later. Your whole drive lives on that card: speed, miles, and time,
glanceable at a red light.

🔧 **Reads your engine, too.**
Plug in a cheap OBD-II dongle and every trip picks up RPM, coolant temp, throttle, fuel use,
battery voltage, and trouble codes — merged right onto your route. A maintenance log that
writes itself.

🚙 **Rides on your dashboard.**
drivetime speaks **Android Auto** — live speed, RPM, coolant, and battery on the car screen
while you drive, and a "leave by" card when you're parked so you're never late.

🔄 **Updates itself.**
No app-store dance. When a new version lands, drivetime offers a one-tap update and keeps
every setting and every trip.

---

## Your data. Your rules.

drivetime is **local-first.** The entire app — maps, stats, everything — is bundled right in
the download and works with **no server, no signup, and no internet.** Your trips are
computed on your phone and stored on your phone.

Want a backup, or the same trips on your laptop? Flip on **sync** and point drivetime at a
server — one you host yourself, or a simple hosted plan. Sync is something you *choose*,
never a fee you're forced to pay to reach your own data. Export everything to a file any
time and move to a new phone in seconds.

---

## Get driving in under a minute

1. **Install it** — download from `drivetime.jupiterns.org/dl/drivetime.apk`. (Approve the
   install once; every update after that installs itself.)
2. **Tap Start.** That's the whole setup — it's now logging.
3. **Optional:** tap **Car Bluetooth** to pair your stereo, add an **OBD dongle** for engine
   data, or turn on **sync** to back up your trips.

> **On a Samsung, Xiaomi, or OnePlus phone?** If drivetime shows a battery banner, tap it and
> allow the app to run in the background. Those phones love to "sleep" apps — the one thing
> that can make *any* tracker miss a drive. drivetime notices when it's been restricted and
> takes you straight to the fix.
